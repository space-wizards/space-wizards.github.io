Space Station 14
The true remake.

Foreword

Space Station 13 has been around for almost 15 years.
BYOND is terrible and might as well be stuck in the 2000's.
It's been obvious for a long time that we need a remake.

The original remake died.
Then SS14 lived for a month and effectively died.
What was once a bustling 100 people IRC channel on Rizon was reduced to 15 people whose join and leave messages are the only thing filling space (bus).
There have been absolutely uncountable attempts. All have failed one way or another.

But giving up isn't the solution.
We're gonna get this game off BYOND. And fix it at the same time.

We're gonna fix the clunky controls. The laggy UI. The lack of a proper Linux client. The terrible development experience.

Space Station 14 is alive and kicking again.
Let's finish this.

The plan

Well, obviously, a remake. Free from the shackles of BYOND. But not just that.

Current SS13 is clunky and annoying to play. This is heavily due to BYOND. Not only do we want to get us off BYOND, we also want to get the game off of it.
No more clunky movement. Pixel perfect combat. Probably a rotating station. A station more scary and dark than Dead Space? Sure!

We can't do this alone though. Neither Rome nor SS13 was built in a day. Did you know SS13 has ~400,000 lines of code?

The Road Ahead

The current tasks at hand are as follows:
  • Sieve through oldcode and remove and fix stuff.
  • Rework the rendering engine to be properly cross platform.
  • Implement a proper scripting system and get rolling on content.
Right now, we consider the code in a "pre-alpha" state. When we finish these tasks, we will move into "alpha" and start following rules such as properly versioning the game.

When we hit alpha we will also rework this website to have monthly development updates.
